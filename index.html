Welcome to Bluehill Central Academy

At Bluehill Central, we are transforming the education experience. We believe every student deserves high quality education. And hence we deliver quality and dedicated lessons taught by our teachers who are 'Trained Practitioners' in the classroom, we ensure that every student gets the best at our academy.

Here at Bluehill, we understand the struggles that students might be facing, or experiencing - we support not sanction. We are here for every single pupil that joins us. Our behaviour ladder is completely different to what other schools use which makes us unique. We allow students to develop as young adults.

At Bluehill Central, we are not just about lessons. We are about experience. We ensure that every student has a memorable experience here at Bluehill. Our Staff team are dedicated at ensuring that all students have the same opportunities as anyone. Hence we train our staff to teach and deliver equipped and well lessons.

Whether you're a former pupil a new pupil or a aspiring pupil we cannot wait to welcome you to Bluehill where you will learn and reach your full potential.

Academy Expectations

We take our guidelines here at Bluehill Central Academy very seriously. Not following our guidelines will result in a disciplinary action - depending on what you have done.

Respect Everyone

At Bluehill Central, we expect students and staff to respect each other with respect. We do not tolerance any sort of disrespect from students or staff, and if seen you will be sanctioned.

Non Unauthorised Advertising

Advertising without permission is completely prohibited. This includes sending any advertising message in (DMs) this includes sending advertisements through our channels. If you want to set an alliance make sure to meet the requirements and create a ticket.

Privacy

Sharing, engaging, threatening or sending personal information (Doxxing) is not allowed and prohibited, and will result in a blacklist or ban. Or any necessary measures we have to take. This includes threats or harassment involving personal data, which is also a violation of Discord terms of service.

Content Appropriation

Posting or sharing inappropriate or prohibited material including NSFW content, graphic violence or any disturbing images is not allowed in our server or chats whatsoever. Doing so will result in you being removed from our community.

Terms Of Service

Bluehill Central follows both Discord and Roblox term of services. All members are expected to follow the term of service also. Violations will result in a consequence depending what you have done.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Bluehill Central Academy - a place where students thrive and grow as young individuals. As Principal, I'm glad to be leading a school full of talented and dedicated students who want to receive a quality education.

At Bluehill Central we believe that every student deserves a right to learn. We train our young individuals to develop their skills and progress into higher education. We train our staff to be excellent practitioners in the classroom and deliver world class lessons.

We follow a realistic manner, which means we take ideas and inspirations from real life schools. We are Proud to be serving East Manchester. We deliver exceptional services to the community and is recognised as a 'Outstanding' school.

Our academy recruit exceptional staff who believes that pupils deserve a quality education, in any circumstances. We train our staff to be trained Practitioners in the classroom and deliver a 'Holistic Learning approach.'

Our roleplay system is very realistic. Our staff are trained to teach in a realistic manner. And our school is a realistic environment. Staff start off as a 'ITT' and qualify with QTS. Then start their 'ECT' which can last for 3-5 weeks. After that staff are Fully Qualified and are given a Qualification that allows them to apply for Middle Leadership Roles.

We hope we have convinced you about our school and we cannot wait to welcome you soon.

Warm Regards,

Adrian Costello
